1 Background

The purpose of this analysis is to investigate the relationship between PFAS exposure during pregnancy and birth weight. We use meta-analytic techniques to summarize epidemiological findings from 27 studies.

2 Overall Meta-Analysis

We ran a random effects model to capture any between-study heterogeneity. The model employs weighted estimation with inverse-variance weights.

2.2 Publication Bias

The Egger’s regression test showed no significant relationship between the observed effect sizes and their standard error (p = 0.3102) indicating no evidence of funnel plot asymmetry due to publication bias.
